The Format

Open Space Technology

The work sessions run onOST — a format where the team sets the agenda, not leadership. Here's how it works.

Facilitator: Peter (Tuesday & Wednesday). Thursday's sessions are team-led (facilitator TBD).

OST works by opening a "marketplace" at the start of each day — anyone can propose a topic, a problem, or a question. The group self-organizes into sessions. If your session is thriving, stay. If not, move. The Law of Two Feet: you're responsible for your own learning and contribution.

The Four Principles

Principle 01
Whoever comes is the right people.
Don't worry about who isn't in the room. The people present are exactly who need to be there.
Principle 02
Whatever happens is the only thing that could have.
Be present to what is, not what you expected. The best outcomes are usually unplanned.
Principle 03
Whenever it starts is the right time.
Creative energy doesn't follow a clock. When the conversation is ready, it begins.
Principle 04
When it's over, it's over.
Don't stretch a dead session. Move on when the energy moves on. The Law of Two Feet applies.

Retreat Objectives

Build Real Team Culture
Move from "people who work together" to a team that trusts each other and has each other's backs.
Strategic Alignment
Every team member understands where Tiger Tracks is headed and how their work fits the bigger picture.
Celebrate Wins
Acknowledge the hard work, the growth, and the people who made it happen. Everyone here has earned their seat.
Q2 Planning & Accountability
Leave Miami with clear priorities, defined ownership, and a shared scoreboard for the next 90 days.
Surface the Hard Conversations
Create space for topics that don't get addressed on Slack or in 30-minute Zooms. The real stuff.

Code of Conduct

Phones Away During Sessions
We have dedicated client work blocks each day. During sessions, be fully present.
Respect Every Voice
This team has a wide range of experience. Every opinion matters. Listen as much as you speak.
Be Present
You flew to Miami for a reason. Engage, participate, lean into the experience.
Confidentiality
What's shared in sessions stays in sessions. Create a safe space for honest conversation.
Have Fun
This is Miami. We're here to work hard AND enjoy each other's company. The best team building happens organically.

Getting Around

Uber / Lyft
Default transportation. Prices are reasonable in Brickell. Share rides when heading to the same place — coordinate in the group chat.
Walking
Brickell Pipelines is 6 min on foot. Brickell City Centre is 2 min. Mary Brickell Village is 5 min. Mostly walkable.
Metromover
Free elevated rail through downtown and Brickell. Good for quick hops. Brickell station nearby.
Brickell Key Walk
Beautiful waterfront loop (1.5 miles) on Brickell Key, 5 min from hotel. Morning run or evening stroll.
